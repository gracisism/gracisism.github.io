severus snape

professor snape

severus snape was the potions master at hogwarts and head of slytherin house

he seemed to greatly despise harry potter during most of his years at hogwarts, but he actually didn't

he had a very dark past, as both his parents mistreated him and his love interest lily, harry's mom, did not like him

severus snape was recruited by lord voldemort in the first wizarding world, but he soon regretted his choice after lily was killed

over the years, severus secretly helped spy for dumbledore and protected harry from the dangers of the dark side

his truth was not revealed until his tragic death, when he was killed by nagini to increase voldemort's power
